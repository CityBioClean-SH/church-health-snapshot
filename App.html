Welcome to Church Health Snapshot

North Texas Assemblies of God

This tool helps assess the overall health of your church across key areas. Here's how it works:

1
Enter your church info — name, pastor, and basic details at the top of the form.
2
Answer 29 questions across three sections: Statistical, Demographics, and Subjective. Your progress is tracked at the top.
3
Submit for your report — you'll receive a health score (out of 95), a rating (Healthy, Caution, or Critical), and specific recommendations.
4
Your answers auto-save — you can close the browser and come back to finish later. Nothing is lost.
